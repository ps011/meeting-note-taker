Detailed Usage

Initial Setup

1. Configure Obsidian Vault

Set the path to your Obsidian vault where meeting notes will be saved. This can be done in the app's settings.

2. Choose Whisper Model

Select the appropriate Whisper model based on your needs:

  • Tiny: Fastest, least accurate (good for quick notes)
  • Base: Balanced speed and accuracy (recommended)
  • Small: Better accuracy, slower processing
  • Medium: High accuracy, much slower
  • Large: Best accuracy, slowest processing

3. Select Llama Model

Choose your preferred summarization model (llama3 is recommended for best results).

Recording a Meeting

Before Recording

  • Ensure your microphone is working properly
  • Test audio levels to avoid clipping
  • Close unnecessary applications to free up system resources
  • Make sure Ollama service is running (if using local LLM)

During Recording

  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Avoid background noise when possible
  • Monitor the recording indicator to ensure audio is being captured
  • Note any important timestamps for later reference

After Recording

  • Wait for processing to complete (this may take several minutes)
  • Review the generated transcription for accuracy
  • Check the summary for completeness
  • Save or organize the notes in your Obsidian vault

Understanding the Output

Transcription

The raw text output from Whisper, containing everything that was spoken during the meeting. This includes:

  • Speaker identification (when possible)
  • Timestamps for different segments
  • Punctuation and formatting
  • Filler words and pauses

Summary

An AI-generated summary created by Llama, including:

  • Key discussion points
  • Decisions made
  • Action items
  • Important details

Best Practices

Audio Quality

  • Use a good quality microphone or headset
  • Record in a quiet environment when possible
  • Test your setup before important meetings
  • Consider using external microphones for better quality

Meeting Preparation

  • Inform participants that the meeting is being recorded
  • Ensure all speakers are audible
  • Have a backup recording method for critical meetings
  • Test the app with a short recording before important meetings

Post-Processing

  • Review transcriptions for accuracy
  • Edit summaries to add context or corrections
  • Organize notes with tags and folders in Obsidian
  • Share relevant sections with team members

Advanced Features

Custom Models

You can use different Whisper and Llama models by modifying the settings. This allows you to optimize for your specific use case.

Batch Processing

For multiple recordings, you can process them sequentially. The app will queue recordings and process them one by one.

Integration with Obsidian

Meeting notes are automatically saved with proper formatting and can be linked to other notes in your vault using Obsidian's linking features.
